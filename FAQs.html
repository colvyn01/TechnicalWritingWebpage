Q: What is the significance of the C1 modem in the iPhone 16e?
A: The C1 modem is Apple's first in-house cellular modem, offering improved power efficiency and better integration with Apple's ecosystem.

Q: How does the iPhone 16e compare to other budget iPhones?
A: The iPhone 16e offers a more powerful A18 chip and enhanced camera capabilities compared to previous budget models.

Q: Is the iPhone 16e compatible with all carriers?
A: Yes, the iPhone 16e supports a wide range of carriers worldwide, thanks to its advanced C1 modem.
