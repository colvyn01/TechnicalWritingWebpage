iPhone 16e

Apple's New Budget
Powerhouse.

The iPhone 16e is Apple's latest budget-friendly smartphone, featuring the A18 chip and Apple's first in-house cellular modem, the C1 chip. This device offers a blend of affordability and performance, making it an attractive option for those looking for a quality iPhone experience without the premium price tag.

Key Features

Apple C1 cellular modem
A18 chip with improved performance
Enhanced camera capabilities
Longer battery life
